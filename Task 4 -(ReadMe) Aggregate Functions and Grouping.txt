Task 4 - Aggregate Functions and Grouping

Description::-

use of SQL aggregate functions and grouping (SUM, COUNT, AVG, GROUP BY, HAVING) on the Ecommerce database.

SQL Queries :
-----------------------
1. SUM: Calculates total quantity ordered per order (from OrderDetails).
2. COUNT: Counts the number of orders placed by each customer (from Orders).
3. AVG: Finds the average stock available for products (from Products).
4. GROUP BY: Groups products by stock value and counts them.
5. HAVING: Filters orders where the total quantity is more than 1.
